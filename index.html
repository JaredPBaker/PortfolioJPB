ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Jared – a passionate marketing professional with a knack for turning creative ideas into tangible results. My journey in the world of marketing has been an exciting blend of traditional and digital strategies, always with an eye on the bottom line.

From my early days spreading magic at Walt Disney World to my current role as a marketing consultant, I've honed my skills in understanding what makes customers tick. This insight has been the cornerstone of my approach: crafting marketing strategies that don't just look good, but deliver real, measurable impact.

My experience spans across various facets of marketing. At Living In Pensacola Group, I implemented content marketing strategies that shifted the focus from selling homes to selling a lifestyle – a subtle but powerful change that resonated with potential homebuyers. I've managed automated email campaigns that speak to customers on a personal level, and I've dived deep into SEO, turning websites into lead-generating powerhouses.

During my time at D.R. Horton, I took on challenges that tested my creativity and analytical skills. I led a campaign that boosted our Google My Business rating from 3.0 to 4.6 stars in just three months – a feat that surpassed our 12-month goal in a single quarter. I've created marketing materials that not only look great but also adhere strictly to brand guidelines, ensuring a consistent and professional image across all platforms.

But it's not just about the numbers. I believe in the power of collaboration and continuous improvement. Whether it's leading cross-functional teams, implementing project management software to boost efficiency, or training sales teams on new CRM systems, I'm always looking for ways to enhance how we work and communicate.

When I'm not immersed in marketing strategies, you'll find me exploring the beautiful hiking trails of Washington with my dog, Ivar, or cheering for the Seattle Mariners (Tridents Up!). As a transplant from Florida, I've fallen in love with the Pacific Northwest and can't imagine calling anywhere else home.

I'm excited about the future of marketing and the endless possibilities it holds. If you're looking for someone who can bring fresh ideas, data-driven strategies, and a passion for results to your team, I'd love to connect. Let's explore how we can take your marketing efforts to the next level and drive real, sustainable growth for your business.
